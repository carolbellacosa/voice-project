The Solution

VOICE is to AI transformation what Agile is to software development

The Protocol

Four roles. Four deliverables. Four career pathways.

Weeks 1-3

Knowledge Cartographer

Document your expertise using AI-guided prompts. When do you escalate? How do you read customer cues? Which workarounds actually work? What patterns do you recognize?

Deliverable
Expertise Map
→ AI Trainer $84K median · 26% growth through 2033
Weeks 4-6

AI Auditor

Test AI tools on your actual work. Record what works, what fails, and why. Where does AI miss nuance? When is human judgment essential?

Deliverable
AI Limitation Log
→ AI Quality Specialist 25% annual job growth
Weeks 7-9

Workflow Architect

Design how humans and AI should divide tasks. Create recommendations grounded in your Phase 1-2 findings. What hybrid approaches work best?

Deliverable
Workflow Blueprint
→ Human-AI Workflow Designer Emerging high-demand role
Weeks 10-12

Transformation Guide

Mentor the next cohort through weeks 1-9. Knowledge cascades internally, building permanent organizational capacity.

Deliverable
Competency Portfolio
→ AI Change Management Critical emerging field

VOICE solves both problems with a single framework

For Organizations

Critical Business Intelligence

VOICE produces three critical outputs that directly inform smarter AI deployment:

  • Knowledge Inventory: Document expertise before it walks out the door
  • AI Readiness Assessment: Validated data on where AI actually works versus fails
  • Implementation Roadmap: Worker-designed recommendations for human-AI task allocation
For Workers

Marketable Skills & Agency

Each role builds competencies for growing career paths and produces tangible proof of capabilities:

  • Skills employers are hiring for: AI training, quality assurance, workflow design, change management
  • Portable credentials: Competency Portfolio with concrete artifacts demonstrating capabilities
  • Agency in transformation: Workers shape how AI is implemented, not just experience it
Evidence-Based Design

Grounded in learning science, designed for real-world impact

VOICE operationalizes three foundational learning science frameworks: Piaget's Constructivism (workers build knowledge through direct AI testing), Lave & Wenger's Situated Learning (Phase 4 creates communities of practice where experienced workers mentor newcomers), and Nonaka's Knowledge Externalization (structured reflection transforms tacit expertise into documented credentials).

Why context matters

Generic reskilling programs have 70% dropout rates because they decontextualize learning. VOICE embeds skill-building in real work, with immediate application and social support structures that research shows are essential for transfer.
